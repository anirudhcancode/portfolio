Project 04

AI Companion Application

Most AI chat apps are stateless — every conversation starts from zero. This is an attempt to build something fundamentally different. An AI that remembers you, reads between the lines, and gets better at knowing you over time.

The Problem

What's wrong with existing AI apps?

1

They forget everything

Every conversation starts from scratch. They don't know what you talked about last week, what you're going through, or anything about who you are unless you tell them again every single time.

2

They can't read the room

They respond the same way whether you're excited, exhausted, or having the worst day of your life. There's no awareness of emotional state — just pattern matching on words.

3

They're purely reactive

They only respond when you message first. They never check in, never follow up on something you mentioned last week, never reach out when you've been quiet for a while.

What's Being Built

How this is different

🧠

Semantic Memory

Every conversation is analyzed and memorable facts are stored as vector embeddings. When you mention something, the system retrieves the most semantically relevant memories — not just keyword matches.

🎭

Emotion Detection

A multi-layer pipeline analyzes behavioral signals — message length, punctuation patterns, time of day, vocabulary — and cross-references labeled emotion datasets to infer emotional state before responding.

🎨

Mood-Adaptive UI

As the system infers your emotional state, the entire app's color palette transitions smoothly across 12 distinct mood themes — the interface itself responds to how you're feeling.

⏰

Proactive Check-ins

When you mention something worth following up on — an interview, a hard conversation, waiting on news — the system schedules a message to follow up at the right time without you having to remember.

🌅

Morning Briefing

Starts your day with a natural, conversational summary — live weather, relevant news, and a personalized opener based on what's been going on in your life recently.

🔒

Privacy First

Row-level security ensures your data is completely isolated. Nobody else can ever access your memories, conversations, or emotional history. It's yours only.
