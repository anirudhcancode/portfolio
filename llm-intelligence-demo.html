Project 02 — Extended

LLM Financial Intelligence Platform

An extension of the LLM Financial Analyzer — pulling 10 years of historical stock data for 10 companies, detecting major market events, analyzing earnings sentiment, and predicting next-quarter price reactions using machine learning.

What was built

PHASE 01

Historical Data Pipeline

10 years of daily OHLCV price data for 10 companies pulled via yfinance and stored in PostgreSQL. 28,877 rows total.

PHASE 02

Earnings History

Up to 49 quarters of earnings data per company — EPS actual vs estimate, revenue, net income, and surprise percentage.

PHASE 03

Event Detection & Analysis

Detected 1,849 major price events (>5% single-day moves) across all companies. Computed 1, 5, and 30-day price reactions after each earnings release.

PHASE 04

Predictive Model

GradientBoosting classifier trained on 435 labeled samples predicts next-quarter sentiment. Regression model predicts 30-day price reaction after earnings.

PHASE 05

API & Visualizations

9 FastAPI endpoints serving historical data, predictions, and correlation analysis. 14 interactive Plotly charts generated automatically.

Visualization 01
